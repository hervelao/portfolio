First post, and the dream to excel

Posted on November 30, 2019

Months ago, with nearly 200,000 entrants around the world, I put myself through the agony of the CFA examinations, for the third and fortunately last time.

The three rounds of exams are known to be tough (around 55% of candidates are likely to fail at each stage of their attempt). Just like many, the CFA exams monopolized my days and nights for almost two years. I spent over 1,000 hours of study, attempting to pass them on first attempt... It was one of the most challenging but rewarding steps I have ever taken, that required strong discipline.

Nevertheless, the road to reach my goal to advance in my investment career is far to be over. It is important to keep in mind that the finance industry is an extremely fast-moving one. Indeed, employers are beginning to prefer practical tech skills as they recruit recent graduates and thus there is a growing demand for it among young professionals in order to stay relevant and competitive.

Growing demand for practical tech skills

It is something that I had clearly observed. Technical knowledge that was missing from me, from AI to big data and robo-advice... And much more are to come. Knowing this, I chose to learn to code.
